# Financial Records, Reporting, and Fiscal Year

## Fiscal Year

The Association's fiscal year **commences January 1 and ends December 31** (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 4).

## Books and Records

The Association keeps correct and complete books and records of account, minutes of Board and committee proceedings, and at its registered or principal office a record of names and addresses of Directors. **All books and records may be inspected by any Director, agent, or attorney for any proper purpose at any reasonable time** (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 1).

## Auditor

The Board may appoint a CPA or CPA firm as auditor. The auditor must not be an Officer and must have no interest in any Lot (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 2).

## Annual Financial Statement

Within **90 days after fiscal year end**, the Board distributes to each Member (and any mortgagee on request) the annual financial statement (balance sheet and income/expense statement) for the preceding fiscal year (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 2).

## Documents Available to Members and Mortgagees

The Association makes available to Members and to mortgage holders/insurers/guarantors, **upon request during normal business hours**, current copies of:
- (a) the Declaration, Articles of Incorporation, Bylaws, and rules
- (b) the most recent financial statement
- (c) the current operating budget
- (d) all other Association records

The Association may charge a reasonable fee for furnishing copies (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 2).

## Documents for Prospective Purchasers

Upon written request of a prospective purchaser of a Lot, the Association makes the same documents available for examination and duplication during reasonable hours (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 2).

## Tax Returns

The Board causes necessary income tax returns to be filed (Source: Bylaws Article XI § 3).

## Contracts, Checks, Deposits

- The Board may authorize officers/agents to enter contracts and execute instruments on behalf of the Association (Source: Bylaws Article IX § 1).
- All checks, drafts, vouchers, notes, or evidences of indebtedness must be signed by such officer/agent and in such manner as the Board determines (Source: Bylaws Article IX § "Section. Checks").
- All Association funds not otherwise employed are deposited in banks/depositories selected by the Board (Source: Bylaws Article IX § 3).

## Stock and Dividends

The Association does **not have or issue shares of stock**. **No dividends** are paid; **no income is distributed** to Directors, Officers, or Members. Reasonable Officer compensation may be paid only as authorized by Articles, Declaration, Bylaws, or Board resolution (Source: Bylaws Article VII).

## Loans to Directors and Officers

**Prohibited.** Directors who vote for or assent to such a loan, and any participating Officers, are jointly and severally liable to the Association for the loan amount until repayment (Source: Bylaws Article VIII).
