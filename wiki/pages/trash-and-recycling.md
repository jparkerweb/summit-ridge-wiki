# Trash and Recycling

## Storage and Containers

- All garbage and other waste must be kept in **appropriate sanitary containers for proper disposal** (Source: Rules and Regulations § Rubbish and Trash; CC&Rs Article V § 1.G; CC&Rs Article VII § 2.D).
- **Trash and recycling bins must be kept in the garage or out of public view, except for day of collection** (Source: Rules and Regulations § Rubbish and Trash).
- Decks may **not** be used as storage areas for garbage/recycling bins (Source: Rules and Regulations § Architecture — Decks/Patios).
- Service yards (garbage, fuel tanks, clotheslines, etc.) must be **completely screened so that the screened elements are not visible at any time** from the street or any adjoining property (Source: CC&Rs Article VII § 2.F).

## Set-Out Timing

All items for trash and recycling are to be put out **no sooner than the evening prior to collection** (Source: Rules and Regulations § Rubbish and Trash).

## Dumping Prohibited

No Lot or part of the Common Area shall be used as a **dumping ground** for rubbish, garbage, or trash (Source: CC&Rs Article V § 1.G; Article VII § 2.D).

All incinerators or other equipment for storage or disposal must be kept in a clean and sanitary condition and comply with all local, state, and federal requirements (Source: CC&Rs Article V § 1.G).

## Enforcement

| Offense | Action |
|---|---|
| **First** | Written warning of violation. **If conditions under CC&Rs Article VII § 2(D) exist for longer than 10 days after written notice**, considered a second offense. |
| **Second / Consecutive** | Written notice of violation and fine of **$10.00 per day** until remedied |

(Source: Rules and Regulations § Rubbish/Trash — Enforcement Procedures)

The CC&Rs also state that if any default under Article VII § 2.D exists for longer than **ten days** after written notice, the Association has all rights and remedies under the Declaration or at law or in equity (Source: CC&Rs Article VII § 2.D).

## Common Service

The Association **may, but is not required to,** arrange as an Association expense with third parties to furnish trash collection and other common services to each Lot (Source: CC&Rs Article XII § 2).
