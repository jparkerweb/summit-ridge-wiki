# Neighbor Disputes

If you become involved in a dispute with a neighbor and are unable or uncomfortable resolving the problem directly with the neighbor, **contact the managing agent for assistance**. They are experienced in handling neighbor-to-neighbor disputes and will counsel you on the proper course of action (Source: Rules and Regulations § Neighbor Disputes).
