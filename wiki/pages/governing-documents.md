# Governing Documents

The SummitRidge Townhomes HOA is governed by a hierarchy of documents. In any irreconcilable conflict, **applicable law and the Declaration control over the Bylaws, amendments, and any rules or regulations** (Source: Bylaws Article XXI).

## Document Hierarchy

1. **Oregon Planned Community Act** (ORS 94.550 to 94.783) and the Oregon Nonprofit Corporation Act
2. **Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions (CC&Rs)** — recorded December 17, 1999 in Multnomah County, Recording No. **99-225640** (Source: CC&Rs § 1; Bylaws Article I § 1.2)
3. **Bylaws** — adopted by the Board on **May 22, 2002**; certified by Secretary Pamela McKenna (Source: Bylaws Certification)
4. **Articles of Incorporation** — filed May 23, 2002 (Source: Second Amendment 2017 § B)
5. **Rules and Regulations** — adopted by the Board under Bylaws Article XIV; current handbook is **Summitridge Townhomes HOA Community Handbook v1.2**

## Amendment Procedures

### CC&Rs (Declaration)
- During first 20 years from recording (12/17/1999): amendment by instrument signed by **not less than 75%** of Owners (Source: CC&Rs Article XI § 1).
- After first 20 years: amendment by instrument signed by **not less than 66-2/3%** of Owners (Source: CC&Rs Article XI § 1).
- **No provision pertaining to the Private Open Space Tracts may be amended or revoked without the written consent of the City** of Lake Oswego (Source: CC&Rs Article XI § 1).
- Amendments materially affecting voting, assessments/liens, reserves, insurance, common-property rights/maintenance, lot boundaries, leasing, right of first refusal, or mortgagee-benefit provisions also require approval of Eligible Holders of first mortgages on at least **51%** of mortgaged Lots, plus 75% Class A votes (Source: CC&Rs Article XIII § 3.B).
- Termination of the Association requires approval of 75% Class A votes, Declarant (so long as Declarant owns land), Eligible Holders of 75% of mortgaged Lots, and **prior written consent of the City** (Source: CC&Rs Article XIII § 3.A).

### Bylaws
- Amendable by majority of Directors present at any regular or special meeting with at least **two days' written notice** of intention and a copy/summary (Source: Bylaws Article XVII).
- The Board has **no authority** to amend or repeal Bylaws provisions relating to election, qualifications, powers, duties, or terms of Directors without Member approval at a special meeting called for that purpose (Source: Bylaws Article XVII).
- No special declarant right may be amended without Declarant's consent (Source: Bylaws Article XVII).

### Rules and Regulations
- Adopted by majority vote of Board members present at a quorate meeting after notice as provided in the Bylaws (Source: Bylaws Article XIV).
- Notice must include verbatim copy of proposed rules; copies must be delivered or mailed to each Member before adoption and after adoption (Source: Bylaws Article XIV).
- Any rule that conflicts with the Bylaws or Declaration is **null and void** (Source: Bylaws Article XIV).
