## SECOND AMENDMENT TO DECLARATIONS OF COVENANTS, CONDITIONS AND RESTRICTIONS OF SUMMITRIDGE TOWNHOMES, LLC

This Second Amendment to Declarations of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions of Summitridge Townhomes, LLC is made this 22nd day of September, 2017 by the Summitridge Townhomes Home Owners Association ("Association") an is effective on recording.

### RECITALS

A. Summitridge Townhomes ("Condominium") is a planned community located in Lake Oswego, Oregon, established pursuant to the following documents recorded in the Records of Multnomah County, Oregon:

1. Declarations of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions of Summitridge Townhomes, LLC, recorded December 17, 1999, as Document No. 99-225640 ("Declaration"); and
2. First Amendment to Declaration, Summitridge Townhomes Homwoeners Association, recorded September 2, 2016, as document 2016-110181.

B. Association is the Summitridge Townhomes Home Owners association, an Oregon non-profit corporation formed pursuant to the Declaration, Bylaws, and Articles of Incorporation, filed May 23, 2002 in the office of the Oregon Secretary of State, Corporation Division.

C. Pursuant to ORS 94.590, the Association and lot owners wish to amend the Declaration in the manner set forth below.

**NOW, THEREFORE,** in accordance with the Declaration and ORS 94.590, with the consent or approval of owners of at least seventy-five percent (75%) of the lots, the Association hereby amends the Declaration in the manner set forth below.

### I. Article IV, Section 8 A is amended to read as follows:

A. If an assessment is not paid on the due date hereinabove set forth, such assessment shall become delinquent and shall bear interest at the rate of 10% per annum from such due date. If so directed by the Board, the secretary of the Association may file in the office of the Multnomah County Clerk within 90 days after such delinquency, a statement of the amount of the delinquent assessments together with interest and notice of the lien of the Association securing such obligation, and upon payment in full thereof shall execute and file a release of such lien. Such assessment with interest as set forth above, together with all costs and attorney fees incurred by the Association in connection with the collection of the delinquency, whether or not suit is filed, shall constitute a Lien on the applicable Lot and shall constitute a personal obligation of the Owner. The Association may bring an action at law to enforce payment of a delinquent assessment against the Owner personally obligated to pay the same and may enforce such lien in the manner provided by law with respect to a lien on real property.

No Owner may waive or otherwise escape liability for assessments by non-use of the Common Properties or abandonment of his Lot.

### II. Article IV, Section 8 B(3) is amended to read as follows:

(3) **Suit or Action.** The Association may bring an action to recover a money judgment for unpaid Assessments, fines and charges under this Declaration without foreclosing or waiving the lien described in this Article. Recovery of funds on any such action, however, shall operate to satisfy the lien, or the portion thereof, for which recovery of funds is made.

### III. Article IV, Section 8 D is amended to read as follows:

D. **Interest, Expenses and Attorneys' Fees.** Any amount not paid to the Association when due in accordance with this Declaration shall bear interest from the due date until paid at a rate of 10 percent per annum. A late charge may be charged for each delinquent assessment in an amount established from time to time by resolution of the Board not to exceed 30% of such assessment. In the event the Association shall file a notice of lien, the lien amount shall also include all costs and attorney fees incurred by the Association in connection with the collection of the delinquency, whether or not suit is filed. In all cases, the Association shall be entitled to recover from an Owner all costs and expenses, including attorney fees, incurred in connection with the collection of money due or the enforcement of this Declaration.
