# Summit Ridge HOA Parking Regulations Reminder

> Converted from `590 HOA Parking Regulations 01.16.2020.pdf` on 2026-05-10.
> Source of truth: the PDF was the original; this markdown is a verbatim transcription.

All Summit Ridge HOA residents and homeowners are respectfully requested to please review and adhere to the HOA Handbook, page 12, parking rules and regulations copied below for your reference. Guest parking spaces are not to be used by homeowners other than short-term temporary situations and with the approval of the board.

## Parking

In order to assure equitable parking and access to the community the following policy has been established. Permissible vehicles: Vehicles that may be parked within the community include conventional passenger vehicles in good repair and that are currently licensed and in regular use.

**Prohibited Vehicles and Equipment:** The following vehicles may not be parked in the community, except for loading or unloading of vehicle:

- Trailer
- Camper
- Tent Trailer
- Boat
- Motor Homes
- Recreational Vehicles (RV's)

Please refer to Mt. Park CC&R Article VII Section 1B. Loading or unloading of prohibited vehicles and equipment is permitted subject to a 24-hour time limit.

**Garage Use/Resident and Guest Parking:** Garages are to be used for vehicle, trailer, camper or boat parking and cannot be totally converted to storage and/or living space. If occupant owns one vehicle, that vehicle must be garaged except for short-term parking on the driveway.

If the occupant owns, more than one vehicle, one vehicle must be garaged and the other vehicle(s) parked on the driveway. The second vehicle is to be parked in your driveway to leave parking spaces in the common area designated for guests.

**If the occupant has a third vehicle, the Board must approve the parking of this vehicle in the community.**

Garage Doors should remain closed except to have access to garage areas.

**Curb Side / Street Parking:** To allow emergency vehicles and owners access to driveways, there is no curbside / street parking within the community.

Violations of the HOA Parking Regulations will result in fines, ticketing by City of Lake Oswego Police Department and/or towing of vehicles.

## Enforcement Procedures

### Garage Use:

In the event that you are not using your garage for vehicle parking, with exception to the use of trailer, camper and/or boat parking, you may be given a notice to remedy this within 30 days. If not completed during this time period, you will be called to appear at a hearing with the board of directors and possibly fined. A fine of **$200 per month** can be levied against the owner until the violation is remedied.

### Parking:

- **First Offense:** Violation notice shall be attached to offending vehicle. Violation is considered a warning and is to be remedied within 24 hours. If not remedied in this time period, the violation will then be considered a second offense (see below).
- **Second Offense:** Violation notice shall be attached to the offending vehicle. The violation must be remedied within 24 hours or the vehicle will be towed at the owner's expense. Letter from managing agent or Board of Directors to be sent by mail to offending owner.
- **Third Offense:** Towing of offending vehicle will be towed at owner's expense. Towing of a vehicle must be authorized by at least two people, one of which must be a board member.

If more than 30 days passes between the above violations, the offenses recalculate.
