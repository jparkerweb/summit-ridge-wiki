## FIRST AMENDMENT TO DECLARATION — SUMMITRIDGE TOWNHOMES HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION

This First Amendment to the Declaration is made on the date indicated below by Summitridge Townhomes Homeowners Association, an Oregon nonprofit corporation. This amendment is effective upon recording in the Records of Multnomah County, Oregon.

WHEREAS certain real property in Multnomah County, Oregon was subjected to a Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions ("Declaration") recorded on December 17, 1999 as Recording No. 99-225640;

WHEREAS not less than a majority of the Board of Directors of the Summitridge Townhomes Homeowners Association approved the amendment described herein, and, after notice to all of the Association members entitled to vote thereon, not less than 75% of the voting power of the Association consented in writing to the amendment described herein; and

WHEREAS, the Declaration may be amended pursuant to Article XI of the Declaration;

NOW THEREFORE, the undersigned do certify that Article IV, Section 4 of the Declaration is amended to read as follows:

> **SECTION 4. SPECIAL ASSESSMENTS.** Upon vote of the Members of the Association in the manner hereinafter set forth, the Association may levy in addition to annual assessments a special assessment for the purpose of defraying in whole or in part the cost of construction or reconstruction or expected repair or replacement of any component for which the Association is responsible under this Declaration, including necessary fixtures and personal property related thereto.

The terms of this Amendment shall control over and implicitly amend any inconsistent provision of the Declaration or Bylaws. Except as specifically amended by this instrument, all provisions of the Declaration shall remain in full force and effect.
